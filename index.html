Certificate Of MinistryAnd Other Related Courses.

Prerequisites : High school or equivalent qualification subject to an assessment of work, ministerial experience, prior learning experience and / or a diagnostics test to access the academic aptitude, interest and suitability for the undergraduate program. contact pcst for details.

The foundation-level courses form the core of our Certificate Program curriculum and are designed to take approximately 12 months to complete as students progress through them one at a time. The Courses are designed to work together to form a strong foundation of understanding within the Christian faith and are modeled after the core curriculum of Shekinah Grace Bible College's prestigious Diploma of Ministry and Bachelor of Ministry programs.
